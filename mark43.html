MARK 43

        As of my last knowledge update in January 2022, the Mark 43 armor is a fictional powered exoskeleton 
        suit that appears in Marvel Comics and the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). Here is some information 
        about Iron Man Mark 43 based on the MCU:

        1. First Appearance:
            - The Mark 43 first appeared in the film "Avengers: Age of Ultron," released in 2015.
                
        2. Design and Features:
            - The Mark 43 is an upgrade from the Mark 42 armor, featuring improved technology and capabilities.
            - It maintains the modular design concept introduced with the Mark 42, allowing the suit to be 
              disassembled and reassembled remotely.
                
        3. Enhancements:
            - Enhanced strength and durability compared to its predecessors.
            - Improved flight capabilities and maneuverability.
            - Upgraded repulsors and other weaponry.
                
        4. Relationship to the Plot:
            - In "Avengers: Age of Ultron," Tony Stark (Iron Man) uses the Mark 43 during the battle against 
              Ultron and his army of drones.
            - The suit plays a crucial role in the film's plot and contributes to the Avengers' efforts to 
              save the world.
                
        5. J.A.R.V.I.S. Integration:
            - The Mark 43 is equipped with J.A.R.V.I.S. (Just A Rather Very Intelligent System), Tony Stark's 
              AI assistant, providing real-time information and assistance.
                
        6. Part of Iron Legion:
            - The Mark 43 is part of the Iron Legion, a group of Iron Man suits controlled by J.A.R.V.I.S.
                
        7. Post-"Age of Ultron":
            - The Mark 43 doesn't play a significant role in subsequent films, as it is often succeeded by 
              newer versions in the MCU.
                
        8. Tony Stark's Continuous Upgrades:
            - Throughout the MCU, Tony Stark is known for continuously upgrading and improving his Iron Man 
              suits, leading to the creation of various versions.
