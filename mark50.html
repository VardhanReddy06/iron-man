MARK 50

        The Iron Man Mark 50 armor is a fictional powered exoskeleton suit that appears in Marvel Comics and 
        the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). It made its first appearance in the film "Avengers: Infinity War"
        released in 2018. Here are some key features and details about the Iron Man Mark 50 armor:

        Nanotechnology: One of the most significant features of the Mark 50 is its use of nanotechnology. The 
        suit is composed of nanobots that form a liquid-like substance, allowing the armor to materialize and 
        dematerialize on Tony Stark's body at will.
              
        Enhanced Abilities: The Mark 50 is equipped with advanced technology that enhances Iron Man's abilities. 
        It provides increased strength, durability, and flight capabilities compared to its predecessors.
              
        Bleeding Edge Armor: In the comics, the Mark 50 is often referred to as the "Bleeding Edge" armor. This 
        term signify its cutting-edge design and use of nanotechnology to form the suit around Tony Stark body
              
        Arc Reactor: Like previous Iron Man suits, the Mark 50 is powered by an arc reactor. However, the 
        technology has been miniaturized and improved, contributing to the suit's sleek design.
              
        Repulsor Technology: The suit is equipped with repulsor technology, which allows Iron Man to fly and 
        project energy blasts from his hands and chest.
              
        Multi-Functional: The Mark 50 is capable of transforming its shape and forming various weapons,  
        including blasters and swords, using the nanotechnology. This adaptability makes it highly versatile in 
        combat situations.
              
        Appearance in "Avengers:Infinity War": In the MCU, the Mark50 played a crucial role in the battle  
        against the villain Thanos and his Black Order in "Avengers: Infinity War." Tony Stark used the suit   
        to confront the powerful enemy and protect Earth.

        Integration with Tony Stark's Extremis: In the comics, the Mark 50 armor integrates with the Extremis 
        technology, a storyline that involved a biological enhancement allowing Stark to interface with  
        technology at a cellular level. This integration enhances Stark's connection with the suit.
